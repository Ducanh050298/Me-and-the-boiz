Group reflection

In general, our group can work together effectively. We not only do well in communication but also support one another. Firstly, we communicate through social media such as Facebook and messenger. Also, we use GitHub to contribute to the team members’ work for easy tracking. Moreover, we are always ready for support when a member gets into trouble. We offer him helps through online and offline methods. However, some points need improving. Firstly, we should increase the number of offline meetings for face-to-face discussion as we are leaning towards online meetings, which may cause some misunderstandings while discussing. Next, we should allocate the tasks more suitably as we can feel the pressure while dividing tasks. At the initial stage, I was afraid of team communication as we hardly talk to one another in the first weeks. However, I have found that our team is one of the most talkative team, enhancing working efficiency. By working together, I have learned a myriad of knowledge from my teammates. One of the essential knowledges is communicating effectively by using body language and spoken languages. While working, GitHub plays an important role in our progress. It helps us commit files and manage them easily. It not only shows how hard we work on this project but also provides us a method to host our website.​ 

Nguyen Luu Quoc Bao

Group reflection

Overall, our group works well until the end of the project. Everything went well according to the plan and the time frame we planned at the beginning of the course. The first thing that I like about this group is that everybody is ready and prepared for the group meeting and prepare a slide for group participation and presentation. Second, although there are some small issues in dividing and giving tasks to all group members, all the people have done their tasks well and finished them before the group deadline. Moreover, not only the group is hard working but also very efficient. The most surprising thing when working with my group is that when there are unexpected problems, the group leader has had that part cover for the whole team. Moreover, from that, working as a group means that we work with each other, if one is not able to finish one’s tasks, then the other would help to cover for them, or to make it more transparent, working in this group has shown me how teamwork is going to help the group develop further. As for activity on GitHub, we use one personal computer to design the website together, so although it is a group repository, we only use one account to commit and push the whole thing up to GitHub. 

Nguyen Trong Minh Long

Group reflection

In this assignment, I am in charge of a part of the report and convert the report into slides. I think I have worked quite well, although there are still many skills I have to learn and more things I have to improve. Overall, I have done a pretty good job. I learn many things when I work with my team on this assignment. First of all, I learn designing skills from Bao and essay-writing skills from both Bao and Long. Second, it is a team working skill, a crucial skill. Lastly, I learn to be opened, share my ideas, thoughts, weaknesses, communicate. Of course, there will be conflicts but what is more important is what you learn from them. 

Nguyen Dang Nhat

Group reflection

Group reflection

Our group has worked very well on this project. Since the first weeks, we have communicated to one another to understand each member's strengths and weaknesses for dividing tasks based on the advantages. While working, there were some conflicts among us. To solve the trouble, we decided to sit together, respectfully discuss the problem and find the optimal solutions to that problem. A clear illustration of this is that when Duc Anh had a family issue, we all helped him complete his parts and gave him some encouragement. In terms of working, our group has a professional attitude by accomplishing the tasks on time and committing the work to GitHub for easy tracking. In addition, all the group members agree that we have learned a significant amount of knowledge, such as self-learning technique and communication skills, which are two essential factors for success in teamwork. Moreover, we can academically develop our skills in HTML, CSS, coding skills, presentation, and slide making. What is more, we can learn from one another in the way they arrange the time for completing the tasks so that we can optimize our schedule. In terms of the effectiveness of the tools, we use GitHub for committing the files. This shows clear effectiveness as we can easily track and share file for working together. Furthermore, GitHub also helps us hosting our group website, which saves a great amount of time. However, there are some aspects that can be improved while working. Firstly, we should meet one another more frequently as we are leaning towards online meetings through messenger and GitHub. Since face-to-face meetings should resolve conflict or unclear knowledge, we should change our communication plan for better products. Besides, we should increase the speed of completing tasks as we have allocated much time to tasks for careful checks. In terms of surprising things, we are all surprised by the communication of the team. At first, all members were quite cooling and preservative, which causes suspicions for the success of the team. However, we realized that all members are outgoing and ready to give help while confronting trouble by working together. Another surprising thing is that Nhat, Bao, and Long can do very well in self-learning and leading the team as they were initially quite cool and shy. However, we can finally overcome all the problems and launch the product, which is pride.
